SIGINT: SIGNAL INTELLIGENCE LOOKUP

Do not fly blind. Use RS (Return Strength) values to identify rock composition before you fly 10km to check it.
RS LOOKUP TABLE (@ 10KM)
1,000 - 3,000 RS
TRASH. Likely single rock < 2000kg. Ignore.
6,000 - 8,000 RS
POSSIBLE. Could be a 5k mass rock or a small cluster. Verify angle.
12,000+ RS
JACKPOT. Guaranteed massive cluster. 2+ Rocks of high mass. BURN TO TARGET.

YIELD CALCULATOR

FORMULA: (MASS × %) ÷ 200 = SCU

Example: 5,000kg Rock @ 40% Quantainium.
5000 × 0.40 = 2000 units.
2000 ÷ 100 (cSCU conversion) = 20 SCU.
RESULT: Fills 62% of Prospector Hold (32 SCU). TAKE IT.

THE 30% RULE: Never crack a rock under 30% Quantainium unless it is massive (>6k). The filtration system cannot separate the inert materials fast enough, filling your bags with trash.
