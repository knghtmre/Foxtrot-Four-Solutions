Ignore these: Only appears inside 3-4km, faint or flickering, resolves into single pebble. Those waste time.

05 // MINING EXECUTION DOCTRINE

Mining operations follow a strict four-phase doctrine. Deviation from this procedure results in lost ore and potential ship loss.

PHASE 1: PROSPECTING

Objective: Locate and identify viable mining targets.

  1. Approach mining zone at 10km+ distance
  2. Activate mining scanner (F key)
  3. Identify scan returns matching criteria (8km+, bright, stable)
  4. Approach target at 5km distance
  5. Perform detailed scan (hold F)
  6. Evaluate rock: mass, ore %, instability, timer impact
  7. Proceed to Phase 2 if rock meets criteria; otherwise, repeat

PHASE 2: FRACTURING

Objective: Break the rock using controlled laser power.

  1. Approach rock at 2km distance
  2. Align with rock surface (perpendicular approach)
  3. Activate mining laser (Left Mouse)
  4. Manage power output: start at 30%, increase to 60-80% as instability allows
  5. Monitor instability meter: keep below 75% if possible
  6. Use gadgets if instability spikes (Stampede for volatility, Surge for power)
  7. Fracture occurs when instability reaches 100% AND power is sufficient
  8. Maintain distance: 1.5-2km from rock center

PHASE 3: EXTRACTION

Objective: Collect valuable ore fragments. QUANTANIUM TIMER STARTS NOW.

  1. Deactivate mining laser immediately after fracture
  2. Activate tractor beam (E key)
  3. Collect high-value fragments first (Quantanium, Taranite)
  4. Ignore inert material and low-value fragments
  5. Monitor cargo hold: target 60-80% fill
  6. Monitor Quantanium timer: 25-minute hard limit
  7. Once target fill reached, proceed to Phase 4 immediately

PHASE 4: REFINEMENT & STORAGE

Objective: Secure ore and initiate refining process.

  1. Set quantum course to nearest refinery (HUR-L1, CRU-L1, or Pyro station)
  2. Quantum jump to refinery
  3. Land at refinery station
  4. Exit ship and access refinery terminal
  5. Initiate refining job for collected ore
  6. Select refining method (standard, optimized, or rush)
  7. Confirm job and wait for completion
  8. Retrieve refined materials and store in personal inventory or sell
TIMER DISCIPLINE: The 25-minute Quantanium timer is absolute. If timer expires before reaching refinery, ore is lost. Plan your mining run to end with 5+ minutes remaining.

06 // MINING LOCATIONS

Three systems contain viable mining zones. Stanton is safest for learning; Pyro offers maximum profit with extreme risk.

STANTON SYSTEM

LYRIA (ArcCorp)
Risk Level: Low
Primary Ores: Quantanium, Corundum, Titanium
Proficiency: Beginner
Nearest Refinery: Shubin SAL-12 (~15km)

Approach Vector: 270 degrees (west), altitude 5-10km AGL. Extreme cold environment; monitor cooler status.

Hazards: Extreme cold, occasional electromagnetic interference. No pirate activity.

Profitability: High. Consistent yields, safe environment. Ideal for learning Quantanium mining without pressure.

YELA ASTEROID BELT (Crusader)
Risk Level: Medium
Primary Ores: Taranite, Hadanite, Bexalite
Proficiency: Intermediate
Nearest Refinery: CRU-L1 (~20km)

Approach Vector: Approach from ecliptic plane, altitude 10-15km AGL. Large asteroid clusters; collision risk present.

Hazards: Pirate activity (occasional), collision risk with asteroids, no armistice zone.

Profitability: Very High. Mid-tier ores offer excellent value. Requires navigation skill.

MICROTECH CLIO MOON
Risk Level: Low
Primary Ores: Quartz, Copper, Corundum
Proficiency: Beginner
Nearest Refinery: New Babbage (~10km)

Approach Vector: Approach from north, altitude 5km AGL. Closest refinery in Stanton.

Hazards: Extreme cold, no pirate activity. Armistice zone active.

Profitability: Medium. Low-value ores, but excellent for learning with zero risk.

PYRO SYSTEM

Risk Level: Extreme

07 // TROUBLESHOOTING & EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

Common mining failures and their solutions. Proper diagnosis prevents lost ore and ship loss.

LASER POWER INSUFFICIENT FOR ROCK FRACTURE
Cause: Rock resistance exceeds current laser output. Power plant insufficient or laser power not maximized.
Solution: (1) Increase laser power to 80-90% output. (2) Activate BoreMax module (+20% power). (3) Use Surge gadget for temporary +30% boost. (4) If still insufficient, ABANDON rock. Rock is too hard for your current setup.
INSTABILITY SPIKES UNCONTROLLABLY
Cause: Rock is too volatile for your skill level or equipment. Instability exceeds management capability.
Solution: (1) Immediately reduce laser power to 30-40%. (2) Activate Stampede gadget (-25% instability). (3) If instability still exceeds 80%, ABANDON rock. Volatile rocks waste time and risk fracture failure.
QUANTANIUM TIMER CRITICAL (5 MINUTES REMAINING)
Cause: Mining run extended too long. Quantanium decay timer approaching expiration.
Solution: (1) IMMEDIATELY cease mining operations. (2) Set quantum course to nearest refinery. (3) Quantum jump to refinery. (4) Land and access refinery terminal. (5) Initiate refining job immediately. (6) If timer expires before refinery access, ore is lost. Plan future runs to end with 5+ minutes remaining.
MINING LASER OVERHEATING
Cause: Sustained high-power mining without adequate cooling. Cooler insufficient or damaged.
Solution: (1) Reduce laser power to 50% or lower. (2) Divert power to coolers (F1 → Power Distribution → increase cooler priority). (3) Mine in short 30-second bursts with 15-second cooling breaks. (4) If overheating persists, return to station for cooler replacement or repair.
POWER PLANT INSUFFICIENT FOR SUSTAINED MINING
Cause: Power plant cannot sustain laser + cooler + shield power simultaneously. Brownout condition.
Solution: (1) Reduce laser power to 60% maximum. (2) Reduce shield strength (F1 → Shields → lower power allocation). (3) Disable non-essential systems (radar, quantum drive). (4) If power still insufficient, return to station for JS-300 power plant upgrade.
SHIELD FAILURE DURING MINING OPERATIONS
Cause: Shield generator damaged or depleted. Asteroid collision or weapon fire.
Solution: (1) If in Stanton (safe zone), continue mining; shield not critical. (2) If in Pyro, ABORT mining and return to station immediately. (3) Land at station and access ship terminal. (4) Repair or replace shield generator. (5) Resume mining only after shields fully operational.
CARGO HOLD OVERFLOW / MANAGEMENT
Cause: Collected more ore than cargo capacity. Attempting to collect fragments beyond 100% hold.
Solution: (1) Monitor cargo percentage constantly during extraction phase. (2) Target 60-80% fill to avoid overflow. (3) If hold reaches 95%, STOP collecting immediately. (4) Proceed to Phase 4 (refinement). (5) Do not attempt to exceed cargo capacity; excess ore is lost.
NAVIGATION / APPROACH VECTOR FAILURE
Cause: Incorrect approach angle, altitude too high/low, or collision course with asteroid.
Solution: (1) Abort current approach. (2) Increase distance to 5km from rock. (3) Realign approach vector perpendicular to rock surface. (4) Reduce altitude to 1.5-2km from rock center. (5) Approach slowly at <100 m/s. (6) If collision imminent, engage reverse thrust and abort.
ENVIRONMENTAL HAZARD DAMAGE (COLD, RADIATION)
Cause: Extreme environmental conditions damaging ship systems. Lyria extreme cold or Pyro radiation.
Solution: (1) Increase cooler power allocation (F1 → Power Distribution). (2) Reduce time in hazardous zone. (3) Mine in short bursts (30 min max) then retreat. (4) If hull integrity drops below 50%, return to station immediately. (5) Repair hull damage before resuming operations.
PIRATE ENCOUNTER (PYRO ONLY)
Cause: Hostile players or AI detected in mining zone. Pyro system has no armistice zone.
Solution: (1) IMMEDIATELY cease mining. (2) Activate quantum drive and set course to refinery or safe zone. (3) If quantum drive offline, engage full thrust away from threat. (4) If combat unavoidable, activate shields and return fire if equipped. (5) Prioritize escape over ore collection. (6) Do not mine in Pyro without combat readiness or group support.
EMERGENCY PROTOCOL: If ship integrity drops below 30%, all systems critical, or pirate contact imminent, ABANDON mining immediately and return to station. No ore is worth losing the ship.

08 // PRE-FLIGHT & POST-FLIGHT CHECKLIST

Complete all items before departure and after return. Skipping checklist items results in mission failure.

PRE-FLIGHT VERIFICATION (Before Undocking)

Ship power plant operational (JS-300 or equivalent)
Quantum drive installed and functional (Atlas or equivalent)
Shield generator operational (FR-66 or equivalent)
Mining laser installed (Helix I or Arbor MH1)
Mining modules equipped (BoreMax, OptiMax if top-tier)
Mining gadgets in inventory (Surge, Stampede)
Cooler operational and set to high priority (Zero-Rush)
Cargo hold empty (0% fill)
Hull integrity at 100%
All ship systems green (no warnings)
Quantum fuel full
Mining location selected and route planned
Refinery location identified for ore delivery

MINING OPERATION VERIFICATION (At Mining Zone)

Mining scanner active and functional
Scan returns visible on HUD
Target rock identified (meets mass/ore % criteria)
Approach vector aligned (perpendicular to rock)
Distance to rock 1.5-2km (optimal fracturing range)
Laser power at 30% (ready to increase)
Tractor beam ready for extraction
Cooler priority set to high

POST-MINING VERIFICATION (After Extraction)

Cargo hold fill between 60-80%
Quantanium timer status checked (if applicable)
Quantum course set to refinery
Quantum fuel sufficient for jump
All mining systems powered down
Ship systems stable (no overheating)

REFINERY ARRIVAL VERIFICATION

Landed safely at refinery station
Refinery terminal accessible
Refining job initiated for collected ore
Refining method selected (standard/optimized/rush)
Refining job confirmed and processing
Cargo hold now empty (ore transferred to refinery)

SHIP MAINTENANCE (Post-Operation)

Hull integrity inspected (repair if <90%)
All ship systems checked for damage
Cooler efficiency verified
Mining laser condition checked
Shield generator operational
Quantum drive functional
All repairs completed before next operation
CHECKLIST COMPLETION: All items must be checked before next mining operation. Incomplete checklist indicates unresolved issues.
